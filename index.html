Welcome to the Serviam Staff Chat

This page is your direct line of communication between the Pharmacy Division and Caregiver Staff. Use the chat window to coordinate care, ask questions, and share updates in real time. No Microsoft Teams license is required — just open this page in any browser.

How to use this chat

💊 Pharmacy Division

  • Use chat to confirm medication orders
  • Notify caregivers of prescription updates
  • Flag urgent medication changes immediately
  • Keep messages clear and concise

🤝 Caregiver Staff

  • Use chat to request medication information
  • Report patient medication concerns
  • Confirm delivery and administration times
  • Escalate urgent issues immediately
